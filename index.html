Abstract 摘要

SmoothConv and DuplexConv are open-source twin datasets of real-world, unscripted Chinese conversations (2,100+ hours), built for full-duplex spoken dialogue and Speech LLM research. Recorded in multi-channel settings across Tutoring and Social Chat, they capture natural turn-taking, overlaps, pauses, and paralinguistic events—not studio readings or scripted speech.

SmoothConv (100h) offers expert human annotations for fine-tuning and evaluation. DuplexConv (2,000h) applies an LLM labeling pipeline at scale for pre-training. This page provides annotation sample videos and dataset distribution charts.

SmoothConv 与 DuplexConv 是面向全双工口语对话与语音大模型研究的开源中文自然对话数据集（2,100+ 小时）。数据采用多通道录制，覆盖 Tutoring 与 Social Chat 场景，保留真实话轮切换、重叠、停顿与副语言信息，而非录音棚朗读或脚本化语音。

SmoothConv（100 小时）提供专家级人工精标，适用于微调与评测；DuplexConv（2,000 小时）通过大模型自动标注流水线支撑大规模预训练。本页提供标注样例视频与数据分布统计图。
